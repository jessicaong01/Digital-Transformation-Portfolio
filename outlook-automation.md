# Outlook Automation Suite

## Overview

Developed a suite of Outlook-based automations to streamline email-driven operational workflows, reducing manual intervention in shipment tracking, document management and reporting activities.

## Features

### Email Monitoring & Workflow Triggering

* Monitors designated mailbox folders for incoming operational emails
* Automatically identifies relevant emails based on predefined business rules
* Triggers downstream automation processes without user intervention

### Attachment Processing & Data Extraction

* Extracts and saves email attachments to structured folder locations
* Processes shipment documentation and PDF files
* Retrieves key business information from email content and attached documents
* Standardises file naming conventions and folder structures

### Hands-Free Shipment Tracking Updates

* Automatically captures shipment-related information from incoming notifications
* Extracts shipment identifiers, tracking details and document references
* Updates shipment tracking workbooks and operational trackers
* Maintains processing logs and audit trails
* Eliminates repetitive manual data entry activities

### Automated Email Generation

* Generates email drafts and notifications based on business events
* Populates email templates using extracted shipment and report data
* Supports stakeholder communication workflows and operational follow-ups

### Automatic Document Filing & Archiving

* Organises files into predefined directory structures
* Archives shipment and operational documents automatically
* Prevents duplicate processing through logging and tracking controls
* Maintains historical records for audit and reference purposes

## Technologies

* Outlook VBA
* Excel VBA
* File System Automation
* PDF Processing
* Email Template Automation
* Workflow Automation

## Business Impact

* Reduced manual email processing effort
* Improved shipment visibility and tracking accuracy
* Accelerated document handling and filing
* Increased process consistency and auditability
* Enabled near hands-free execution of recurring operational workflows
