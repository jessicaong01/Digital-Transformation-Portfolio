# SAP Reporting Automation

## Problem

Manual extraction of weekly operational reports for multiple customer accounts required significant effort beyond report generation. 
After extraction, data had to be cleaned, reformatted, consolidated and transformed into various business-ready outputs depending on the intended audience and use case.

Key challenges included:

- Downloading reports from SAP
- Standardising report formats
- Removing unnecessary data and columns
- Applying business rules and calculations
- Consolidating multiple account reports
- Generating account-specific tracking files
- Preparing weekly master datasets
- Creating customer-facing reports
- Producing operational and management reporting outputs

The process was repetitive, time-consuming and prone to manual errors.

## Solution

Developed an end-to-end reporting automation framework that:

- Extracts reports directly from SAP systems
- Saves files using standardised naming conventions
- Performs automated data cleansing and transformation
- Applies account-specific business logic and calculations
- Consolidates data across multiple customer accounts
- Generates weekly master files used for operational tracking
- Produces customer and supplier-facing reports for account stakeholders
- Creates analytical outputs for operational reviews and management reporting

The solution combined SAP GUI scripting, VBScript and Excel VBA to automate the entire workflow from report extraction through final report generation.
## Results

Generated multiple reporting outputs from a single source dataset, including:

- Weekly master planning files
- Customer and supplier account reports
- Backlog tracking reports
- Gap analysis reports
- Shipment tracking files
- Operational review packs
- KPI and management reporting

This eliminated repetitive report preparation activities while improving consistency, traceability and reporting turnaround times.
