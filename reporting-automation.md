# Reporting Automation & Analytics Engine

## Overview

Designed and developed a reporting automation framework that transforms raw operational data from multiple sources into business-ready analytical outputs, reducing manual report preparation effort while improving reporting accuracy, consistency and turnaround time.

The solution automates the complete reporting lifecycle, from data ingestion and transformation to analysis, exception identification and final report generation.

## Core Capabilities

### Data Ingestion & Consolidation

* Imports data from multiple operational reports and source files
* Consolidates information across customer accounts, business units and reporting periods
* Standardises disparate report formats into a unified data model
* Automates recurring weekly and daily reporting workflows

### Data Cleansing & Transformation

* Removes redundant and non-value-added data
* Applies business-specific formatting and transformation rules
* Standardises dates, quantities, pricing and operational metrics
* Generates calculated fields and reporting attributes
* Creates reusable master datasets for downstream analysis

### Business Logic Automation

* Applies account-specific operational rules and reporting requirements
* Automates classification, segmentation and exception handling
* Generates unique identifiers and matching logic for data reconciliation
* Performs automated validations and consistency checks
* Reduces dependency on manual formula maintenance

### Advanced Analytical Reporting

* Backlog analysis and ageing visibility
* Supply and demand gap identification
* Delivery commitment tracking
* Lead-time and schedule variance analysis
* Shipment and inventory performance monitoring
* Operational exception reporting

### KPI & Performance Monitoring

* Generates operational performance metrics automatically
* Tracks trends, variances and service-level performance
* Produces account-level and consolidated KPI reporting
* Highlights risk areas requiring intervention
* Supports operational review and planning activities

### Dynamic Reporting & Dashboard Generation

* Automated pivot table generation
* Dynamic summary reporting
* Multi-dimensional data analysis
* Customer-specific reporting outputs
* Weekly master planning files
* Exception and escalation reports

### Reconciliation & Data Validation

* Cross-references multiple operational data sources
* Identifies mismatches, exceptions and data integrity issues
* Automates comparison reporting between source systems
* Improves confidence in reporting outputs through validation controls

## Technologies

* Excel VBA

* Workflow Automation

## Business Impact

* Reduced report preparation time from hours to minutes
* Eliminated repetitive manual data manipulation activities
* Improved reporting consistency across multiple accounts
* Increased visibility into operational risks and exceptions
* Enabled faster decision-making through timely and reliable reporting
* Established reusable reporting frameworks that scale across multiple business processes
